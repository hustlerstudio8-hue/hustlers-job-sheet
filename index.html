Terms & conditions

Care for wrap or PPF

  • Hand-wash only with pH neutral soap after installation.
  • Do not pressure-wash your vehicle wrap or PPF.
  • Avoid de-greasers or agents that may harm the wrap or PPF.
  • Do not use soap with wax or detailing chemicals mixed in.
  • Dry with a clean microfiber towel only. Never use an automatic vehicle wash.
  • Best results on vehicles less than 5 years old with factory paint. Repair damage prior to installation.

Window film care

Window tint may appear hazy or show moisture bubbles for up to 30 days after installation. Do not clean the film for 6 days. Keep windows closed for 48–72 hours after installation. Minor trapped particles are industry-standard and concurrent with best practice.

Ceramic coating care

  • Wait 7 days before washing. Hand wash only — no automatic washes.
  • Use pH-neutral soap and the two-bucket method with microfiber towels only.
  • No high-pressure washers directly on coated surfaces.
  • Reapply a ceramic spray every 3–6 months to maintain warranty.

Client responsibilities

Inspect vehicle before leaving — Hustlers is not liable for issues after departure. Remove all personal items. Hustlers is not responsible for theft or damage to vehicles parked outside the garage or after key collection. Follow care guide or warranty will be void.

Disassembly & reassembly

Limited disassembly of body panels, bumpers, doors, hoods or trunks may be required. Clips and tabs may crack in rare cases. Hustlers Car Studio IS NOT responsible for resetting or clearing error codes triggered by panel removal. Warning light clearance is the customer's sole responsibility.
